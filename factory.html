Factory Overview

Our manufacturing facility is located in Lagos Industrial Zone, covering an area of 5,000 square meters. We have separate production lines for solar energy products, home appliances and 3C electronics, with a total production capacity of 50,000 units per month.

All our production processes comply with international quality standards, and we have a dedicated quality control team to ensure every product meets our strict requirements.

  • 5,000 sq.m production area
  • 3 specialized production lines
  • 100+ skilled workers
  • ISO 9001 quality certification
  • Monthly capacity: 50,000 units

Quality Control Process

We implement a 5-step quality control process to ensure product excellence:

  1. Raw Material Inspection: All incoming materials are tested before production
  2. In-Process Inspection: Regular checks during production
  3. Final Inspection: 100% inspection of finished products
  4. Packaging Check: Verify packaging quality and labeling
  5. Pre-Shipment Inspection: Random sampling before delivery

Our QC team uses advanced testing equipment to ensure all products meet international standards and customer requirements.
