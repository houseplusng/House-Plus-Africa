How to Apply

To apply for any of the above positions, please:

  1. Send your updated CV and cover letter to: careers@houseplus-africa.com
  2. Include the job title in the email subject line (e.g., "Application for Production Supervisor")
  3. Provide information about your relevant experience and why you are suitable for the position
