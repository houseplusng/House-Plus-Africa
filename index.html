About House Plus Africa

Based in Lagos, Nigeria, House Plus Africa is a professional manufacturer and supplier focusing on household & kitchen appliances, solar energy products, and 3C consumer electronics.

We provide one-stop OEM & ODM services, wholesale, and bulk export for clients across Africa and global markets with stable production capacity, reliable quality, and competitive prices.

Learn More About Us

House Plus Africa Manufacturing Capabilities

House Plus Africa operates a state-of-the-art manufacturing facility in Lagos, Nigeria. Our factory specializes in the production of home appliances, solar energy products, and 3C consumer electronics. We have the capacity to handle both OEM (Original Equipment Manufacturing) and ODM (Original Design Manufacturing) projects for clients throughout Africa and internationally.

Our product range includes solar panels, solar inverters, lithium batteries, solar street lights, range hoods, gas stoves, air fryers, ovens, water heaters, power banks, Bluetooth earphones, smart watches, and USB cables. All products are manufactured to international quality standards with competitive pricing suitable for African markets.
