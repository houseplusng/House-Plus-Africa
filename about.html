About Us

Your Trusted Manufacturer in Nigeria

Our Story

Founded in 2020, House Plus Africa is a professional manufacturer specializing in home appliances, solar energy products and 3C consumer electronics. We are located in Lagos Industrial Zone, Nigeria, with easy access to major ports and transportation networks.

Our mission is to provide high-quality, affordable products for African households and businesses, with a focus on sustainability and local market needs.

Our Mission & Vision

Mission

To deliver reliable and affordable home appliances, solar products and 3C goods that improve the quality of life for African consumers.

Vision

To become the leading manufacturer of household products in West Africa, recognized for quality, innovation and customer service.

Our Core Values

Quality

We maintain strict quality control standards to ensure every product meets our high expectations.

Integrity

We conduct business with honesty and transparency, building long-term relationships with our customers.

Innovation

We continuously improve our products and processes to meet the evolving needs of the African market.
