Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to Your Most Common Questions

We manufacture three main categories of products:
  • Solar energy products (solar panels, inverters, lithium batteries, solar street lights)
  • Home appliances (gas stoves, air fryers, range hoods, water heaters)
  • 3C electronics (power banks, Bluetooth earphones, smart watches, USB cables)

Yes, all our products are specifically designed and tested to withstand Nigerian environmental conditions, including high temperatures, voltage fluctuations and humidity. Our solar products are optimized for Nigeria's solar irradiance levels, and our home appliances are designed to work with Nigeria's power supply (220V/50Hz).

Yes, we provide detailed product catalogs with specifications and wholesale price lists to serious buyers and distributors. Please contact our sales team via email or WhatsApp to request a copy of our latest catalog and price list.

Our MOQ varies by product category:
  • Solar products: 50 units per product
  • Home appliances: 50 units per product
  • 3C electronics: 100 units per product
  • OEM/ODM customized products: 500 units per product
For larger orders, we offer volume discounts.

We accept the following payment methods:
  • Bank transfer (GTBank, Access Bank, Zenith Bank)
  • Mobile money (Paga, Opay, Palmpay)
  • Cash (for Lagos-based customers only)
  • Letter of Credit (for international orders)
For orders above ₦1,000,000, we require a 50% deposit upfront and the balance before delivery.

Yes, we offer tiered volume discounts for bulk orders:
  • 100-500 units: 5% discount
  • 501-1000 units: 10% discount
  • 1001+ units: 15% discount
Custom discount rates are available for regular customers and large distributors.

Our delivery times are as follows:
  • Standard products in stock (Lagos): 3-5 working days
  • Standard products (other states): 5-7 working days
  • Custom OEM/ODM orders: 2-4 weeks
Delivery times may be longer during peak seasons or due to logistics challenges in certain regions.

Yes, we deliver to all 36 states in Nigeria and the Federal Capital Territory (FCT). We work with reliable logistics partners to ensure safe and timely delivery to all locations, including remote areas. Delivery fees vary by location and order size.

Delivery costs are typically borne by the buyer, except for large orders (above ₦5,000,000) within Lagos, where we offer free delivery. For orders outside Lagos, delivery fees are calculated based on weight, volume and destination. We provide a delivery quote before confirming your order.

Yes, all our products come with a warranty against manufacturing defects:
  • Solar panels: 5-year warranty
  • Solar inverters & batteries: 2-year warranty
  • Home appliances: 1-year warranty
  • 3C electronics: 6-month warranty
The warranty does not cover damage caused by improper use, negligence, or power surges (unless used with our recommended surge protectors).

Yes, we supply genuine spare parts for all our products at competitive prices. You can order spare parts directly from our sales team or pick them up from our Lagos warehouse. We also ship spare parts to other states via our logistics partners.

Yes, we offer both OEM (Original Equipment Manufacturing) and ODM (Original Design Manufacturing) services for all our product categories. OEM involves manufacturing products with your brand logo and packaging, while ODM involves designing and manufacturing custom products based on your specifications.

The lead time for OEM/ODM orders depends on the complexity of the customization:
  • Simple OEM (logo only): 2-3 weeks
  • Full OEM (logo + packaging): 3-4 weeks
  • ODM (custom design): 4-6 weeks (including design and prototype approval)
We provide a detailed timeline after finalizing the product specifications with you.

Yes, we provide product samples for OEM/ODM orders before starting mass production. The cost of samples depends on the product and customization requirements, and this cost is usually deducted from the final order value if you proceed with mass production. Sample delivery time is 7-10 working days.
