About House Plus Africa

What is House Plus Africa?

House Plus Africa is a professional manufacturer and supplier based in Lagos, Nigeria, specializing in home appliances, solar energy products, and 3C consumer electronics. We provide OEM/ODM services, wholesale, and bulk export solutions for clients across Africa and global markets. With our state-of-the-art manufacturing facility and experienced team, we ensure high-quality products at competitive prices.

When was House Plus Africa founded?

House Plus Africa was founded in 2020. Since our establishment, we have grown to become a leading manufacturer in the African region, serving thousands of customers across multiple countries.

Where is House Plus Africa located?

House Plus Africa is located in Lagos Industrial Zone, Lagos, Nigeria. Our facility is equipped with modern manufacturing equipment and quality control systems to ensure the highest standards of production.

OEM/ODM Services

What is OEM/ODM service?

OEM (Original Equipment Manufacturing): We manufacture products designed by other companies. You provide the design, and we handle the manufacturing.

ODM (Original Design Manufacturing): We design and manufacture products based on our own designs. You can customize the products with your branding.

Both services allow clients to get customized products without building their own manufacturing facility.

Can you customize products?

Yes, we offer full customization services including product design, packaging, branding, specifications, and more. Our ODM service is specifically designed for clients who want customized products. Customization requires minimum order quantities and additional lead time. Please contact us to discuss your specific customization requirements.

Purchasing & Orders

What is the minimum order quantity?

Minimum order quantities vary depending on the product type. For most products, the MOQ is 100-500 units. For bulk orders, we offer more flexible terms. Please contact us for specific product MOQ information.

What is the delivery time?

Standard delivery time is 30-45 days from order confirmation. For rush orders, we can accommodate shorter timelines. Delivery time depends on order quantity, product type, and customization requirements. Please contact us for specific delivery schedules.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept multiple payment methods including:
