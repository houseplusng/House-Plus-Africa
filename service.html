Our Services

Comprehensive Solutions for Your Business

What We Offer

We provide a full range of services to meet the diverse needs of our customers, from customized manufacturing to after-sales support.

OEM Manufacturing

We offer OEM (Original Equipment Manufacturing) services for all our product categories. You can customize products with your own brand logo, packaging and specifications.

  • Custom branding and labeling
  • Flexible product specifications
  • Competitive MOQ (Minimum Order Quantity)
  • Strict quality control
ODM Manufacturing

Our ODM (Original Design Manufacturing) services allow you to bring your product ideas to life with our professional design and engineering team.

  • Product design and development
  • Prototype creation
  • Cost optimization
  • Mass production support
Wholesale Supply

We provide wholesale supply of our standard products with competitive pricing and flexible payment terms for distributors and retailers.

  • Competitive wholesale pricing
  • Flexible payment terms
  • Fast delivery
  • Regular stock availability
After-Sales Service

We offer comprehensive after-sales support to ensure customer satisfaction with our products.

  • 12-month warranty on all products
  • Technical support via phone/email
  • Spare parts supply
  • On-site repair service (Lagos area)
